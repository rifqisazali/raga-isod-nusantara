Two brands, one vision — to elevate Indonesia's finest natural commodities to the world stage, honoring tradition while embracing the future.

Our Vision

To be the global benchmark for premium Indonesian commodities, inspiring the world through quality, sustainability, and authentic heritage.

Our Mission

To empower Indonesian farmers, preserve traditional craftsmanship, and deliver products that honor both people and planet — ensuring every purchase creates a positive impact.

Our Values

Integrity in every process. Sustainability in every harvest. Excellence in every product. We believe premium quality flows from genuine care — for land, community, and craft.

Our Heritage

Where Heritage Meets Innovation

Raga Isod Nusantara was founded with a singular passion: to bring Indonesia's extraordinary natural bounty to the world. From the volcanic coffee highlands to the sun-drenched coconut coasts, we seek out the purest, most authentic expressions of this land.

Our journey is interwoven with the stories of thousands of farming families across the archipelago. We don't just source ingredients — we build partnerships, preserve traditions, and create sustainable futures for the communities that make our products possible.
